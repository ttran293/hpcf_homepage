Goal and Motivation

Our goals for this project are to create compelling visualizations that can provide insight into the usage patterns of the HPCF which can assist in the allocation of resources towards the facility and in determining future policies on this shared UMBC community resource.

In this project:

  • We observe the clustersâ€™ management and the usage recorded by SLURM for each month starting in January 2019.
  • We look at how important events such as the COVID-19 pandemic and the addition of the CPU 2021 machine may have affected cluster usage.
  • We generate visualizations for specific time ranges in the data to see if any previously unexplored trends or anomalies can be found.
  • We construct a narrative for the HPCF usage datasets.
